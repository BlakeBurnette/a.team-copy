What is PayHive?

PayHive is an all-in-one business management platform designed specifically for home service businesses like lawn care, cleaning, HVAC, pest control, and more. We combine payment processing, scheduling, customer management, and marketing tools in one easy-to-use platform built for the way you work.

Key Features

  • ACH & Card Payment Processing - Accept bank transfers and cards with competitive rates. ACH payments mean lower fees for your business.
  • Customer Self-Onboarding - Share signup links where new customers register themselves, set up payment methods, and authorize recurring charges.
  • Mobile-First Scheduling - Schedule jobs, manage crews, optimize routes, and track service completion from any device.
  • Automated Invoicing - Generate and send invoices automatically after services are completed.
  • Customer Portal - Give customers access to view service history, upcoming appointments, invoices, and manage their account.
  • Crew Management - Assign jobs, track time, and enable field crews to check in, record service details, and capture photos.
  • Service Records - Document every service with photos, notes, and timestamps for complete service history.

How It Works

  1. Create your PayHive account and set up your services
  2. Share your custom signup link with new customers
  3. Customers self-onboard and set up autopay
  4. Schedule and complete services with mobile tools
  5. Get paid automatically - no chasing invoices
