PayHive Brand Guidelines 1. Logo Usage
• Primary Logo: The PayHive logo consists of the orange hive symbol paired with the wordmark "PayHive" in bold, dark gray type.
• Clear Space: Maintain a clear space equal to the height of the "P" around the logo.
• Backgrounds: The logo works best on white, black, or dark neutral backgrounds. Use the white
version when placed on darker colors.
• Do Not:
• Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo.
• Alter the colors.
• Place on backgrounds with insufficient contrast.
2. Color Palette

3. Typography
• Wordmark & Headlines: Futura Bold
• Body Text: Montserrat (Google Fonts) – a geometric sans-serif that closely matches Futura’s
structure.
• Usage:
• Headlines should be bold, modern, and clear.
• Body copy should emphasize legibility and professionalism.

5. Tone of Voice
• Confident: Speak with clarity and authority.
• Approachable: Simple language, easy to understand.
• Innovative: Highlight PayHive’s role as a forward-thinking fintech partner.
6. Iconography & Graphics
• Icon style should mirror the hive logo: rounded, geometric, bold. • Avoid thin strokes and unnecessary complexity.
• Colors should align with the primary palette.
7. Photography Style
• Bright, modern, and professional.
• Lifestyle-focused: show real people using products.

• Urban fintech feel: sleek environments, technology-driven aesthetics.
8. Brand Summary
PayHive is about trust, speed, and simplicity in financial technology. The brand identity emphasizes:
• Energy & Growth: through vibrant orange hues.
• Trustworthiness: bold typography and balanced layouts.
• Innovation: flexible applications from debit cards to Times Square billboards.
